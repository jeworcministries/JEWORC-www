About JEWORC

A dynamic, Christ-centered ministry in Uganda

Jesus' Worship Centre Ministries (JEWORC) is a dynamic, Christ-centered ministry based in Kampala, Uganda, in the eastern part of Africa.

Founded on August 17, 2019, JEWORC began as a humble evangelism group and prayer altar and has since grown into a thriving church committed to fulfilling Jesus' command in Mark 16:15 â€” "Go into all the world and preach the gospel to every creature."

We believe in:

  • The confession of sins and forgiveness through Jesus Christ
  • Water baptism and the indwelling power of the Holy Spirit
  • Every individual's opportunity to be born again and walk in a transformed life
