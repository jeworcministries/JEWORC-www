Our Leadership

Servants of God guiding this ministry

Spiritual Guidance

Biblical Leadership

Shepherding God's flock with wisdom and humility

At JEWORC, we are blessed with a team of dedicated leaders who shepherd God's flock with wisdom and humility. Our leadership structure follows the biblical model of elders and deacons, working together to fulfill the Great Commission.

We believe leadership in the church is not about power or position, but about serving others as Christ served us. Our leaders are committed to prayer, the study of God's Word, and the care of His people.
