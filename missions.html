Our Approach

Holistic Outreach Programs

Meeting both spiritual and physical needs in communities we serve

Community Crusades

Large-scale evangelistic events featuring powerful worship, testimonies, and gospel preaching that bring entire communities to Christ.

  • Held quarterly in different regions
  • Average attendance: 500-1,000 people
  • Follow-up discipleship for new believers
Upcoming Crusades

Orphan Care Program

Providing education, nutrition, and spiritual guidance to vulnerable children who have lost parents to disease or poverty.

  • Currently supporting over 50 orphans
  • School fees and supplies provided
  • Monthly visitation and mentoring
Sponsor a Child

Medical Missions

Free medical camps offering treatment, medication, and health education to underserved communities.

  • Conducted visits to communities yearly
  • Serves as many people as can per camp
  • Partnership with Christian doctors
